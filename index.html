About

My education began at Pratt Institute where I studied fine art with an interest in medical illustration. Following my creative studies, I began working predominately as a Freelance Artist, broadening my skillset. I had the honor of collaborating on a wide range of projects including digital, architectural, and small business branding and marketing.

I also worked as the Assistant Director and Treasurer of The Bend Academy of Art (501c3). I built and maintained the business's web presence, designed and launched marketing campaigns, and focused on optimizing educational opportunities for the local community. During this time, I began studying the sciences behind aesthetics through my local community colleges. There, I was introduced to neuroscience and computer programming. Preceding the unfortunate closure of The Bend Academy of Art during the Covid-19 pandemic, my academic focus and life's work shifted completely to neuroscience and technology.

Currently, I study Software Development with MITxPRO and Computational Neuroscience at UAZ. I am investigating the brain's fundamental functions while preparing for a career in AI/ML development. Gaining experience as a developer is imperative for the growth of my career. I am passionate about my work and education. I approach challenges and obstacles as opportunities to improve. My work ethic, combined with my adaptability and resourcefulness, enables me to consistently deliver exceptional results even in high-pressure environments. I believe driving progress and achieving success requires growth through innovative solutions.
